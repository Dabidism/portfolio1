Objectives

The objective of this project is to develop an Intelligent Vehicle Gate Pass System that integrates Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) and Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR).


Specific Objectives:

  • Develop a web-based system for managing vehicle access that can:
  • Allow students, employees, and authorized personnel to register online.
  • Enable users to upload required documents or valid identification during online registration.
  • Display available parking space;
  • Categorize vehicle owners based on their level of access;
  • Monitor the number of vehicles that enter and exit the campus;
  • Generate reports for tracking of vehicles using data analytics in the form of graphs and tables.
